Rough math: hitting $3-4k/month at ₪10/day needs only ~35-50 paying users/day averaged over a month — realistic in scale, not "all of Israel." Distribution answer: Facebook parent groups by class/school (a real, active channel in Israel), not Instagram/TikTok (those would target students directly, who are less likely to be the one paying). **Paused, not dropped** — kids are on summer break, no exam pressure right now. Revisit near the school year / exam periods.

## Decision framework (adopted for future ideas too)

Before committing budget/time to any idea:
1. What exists for free already, and what's actually missing?
2. What can't be copied for free? (physical/certified human presence is the strongest moat found today)
3. Do we have a real, warm distribution channel — or would we be starting cold? (this was the recurring failure point across nearly every idea discussed, all session)
4. Is it actually legal/permitted for us to deliver?
