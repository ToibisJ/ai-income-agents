## Decision framework applied to every new idea (established 2026-07-10)

Before committing budget/time to any idea, run it through:
1. **What exists for free, and what's actually missing?** (e.g., CV templates are free everywhere — the gap is *rewriting quality*, not the PDF)
2. **What can't be copied for free?** (physical/certified presence beats any AI tool — this is why the first-aid idea is structurally the strongest *if* the certification question resolves favorably)
3. **Do we have a real distribution channel**, not a cold one? (this killed or stalled almost every idea discussed today — it's the recurring failure point)
4. **Is it actually legal/permitted for us to deliver?** (blocked the first-aid track pending instructor-certification confirmation)
